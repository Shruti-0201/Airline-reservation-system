Travel Tips

1. Pack Light

Take only what you need. This makes moving around easier and avoids extra baggage charges.

2. Keep Important Documents Safe

Carry copies of your passport, ID, and tickets in a different place than the originals.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water, especially during flights and in hot climates.

4. Learn Local Customs

Understanding traditions and etiquette helps you connect better with locals.

5. Keep Emergency Contacts Handy

Save numbers for local emergency services, your embassy, and close family members.

6. Carry a Power Bank

Ensure your phone and devices are always charged, especially during long journeys.

7. Use a Travel Wallet

Store your passport, cards, currency, and tickets in one secure place.

8. Stay Alert

Be aware of your surroundings and keep an eye on your belongings at all times.
